About Dream Planners Group

Building financial security for Singapore families since December 2013.

Our Founder

Meet Jason Ng

Jason Ng
Financial Services Director
Great Eastern Life Assurance Co. Ltd

Jason founded Dream Planners Group in December 2013 as part of Great Eastern Financial Advisers (GEFA) with a simple belief: financial planning should start with understanding people, not pushing products. From day one, the team has been committed to a needs-based approach — taking the time to listen, analyse, and recommend solutions that genuinely fit each client's situation.

"I believe that anyone can turn their dreams into reality if they have the right guiding hands. I lead my team to their destiny with curated training programmes. My dream is to inspire more shining stars who dare to dream!"

— Jason Ng, Financial Services Director
Our Story

How Dream Planners began

Too many families in Singapore feel overwhelmed by financial jargon and high-pressure sales tactics. Dream Planners was built to be different. We focus on education and transparency, empowering our clients to make informed decisions about insurance, savings, and long-term wealth building. Every conversation starts with your goals, not ours.

Over the years, we have grown into a trusted team of advisers who prioritise long-term relationships over quick transactions. Many of our earliest clients are still with us today — not because they have to be, but because they know we genuinely care about their financial wellbeing at every stage of life.

Our Backing

Part of Great Eastern

Dream Planners Group operates under Great Eastern Financial Advisers (GEFA), a subsidiary of Great Eastern — Singapore's oldest and most established insurance company with over 100 years of heritage. When you work with us, you benefit from the strength and stability of a trusted institution, paired with the personalised attention of a dedicated advisory team.

Giving Back

Community Involvement

Making a Difference Beyond Finance

Dream Planners Group believes in giving back to the community that supports us. Our team regularly volunteers with Food from the Heart, Singapore's leading food charity, helping to sort and distribute food to families in need.

We also participate in Operation Rejoice, spreading festive cheer to underprivileged communities. These initiatives reflect our core belief that true financial planning goes beyond numbers — it's about building a better society for everyone.
